The Kart Racing Club is a community of speed enthusiasts who share a passion for kart racing. Founded by a group of racers who wanted to bring together people who love the thrill of the track, our club organizes regular events, training sessions, and competitions for all skill levels.

Whether you're a beginner looking to experience the excitement of karting for the first time, or a seasoned racer ready to push your limits, our club offers opportunities for everyone. Our mission is to promote sportsmanship, skill development, and a fun racing environment for all members.

Track Limitations: Our track is designed for both speed and safety. The total length is 1.2 kilometers, with challenging turns, straight stretches, and safety barriers. Each race is limited to a maximum of 10 racers at a time to ensure fair play and prevent accidents.

Safety Instructions: - Always wear a helmet and safety gear before entering the track.
- Follow the track marshal's instructions at all times.
- Keep both hands on the steering wheel while racing.
- Avoid intentional collisions.
- Respect the race start and finish signals.
- Slow down immediately if a yellow flag is shown.
